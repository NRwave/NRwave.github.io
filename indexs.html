Terms to Know

Standard Error: It represents the average disparity between predicted and actual values, especially for non-linear regression models. It is derived by square rooting the sum of the residuals(differences between predicted and actual values) divided by the number of data points.

R²: Represents the coefficient of determination(how well regression models predict the actual values on the y-axis).

Outlier: data point with a drastic difference in the overall distribution

Ethnicity: Shared cultural characteristics such as language and cultural traditions

Race: Shared physical characteristics such as the physical color of a person.

Racial Diversity Section: For this section increase in rank is 50th, 49th, etc. while decrease in rank is 1st, 2nd, 3rd, etc.

Although it is clear that America is promoting its diversity on the ethnic level, America can tell a different story of the well-being of race. Analysis

Notably, however, struggling states like Alaska(Overall Ranking: 49th) and Hawaii(Overall Ranking: 47th), are 1st and 6th in economic equality, respectively due to their high inclusiveness in industries.
